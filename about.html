SOLVE FOR ONE

In 2014, Experience Designer Matt Reamer built a device to help his brother, whose autism prevents him from being able to speak, communicate his wants and needs with their mother for the first time ever. He named the device after his brother – Dustin’s Words.

Dustin uses the device his brother built for him to tell his mother when he’s hungry, when he has a migraine, and even when he wants to get out of the house for a ride on his scooter.
